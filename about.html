📖 Our Story

From a Weekend Project to India's Most Trusted Calculator Site

Needful Calculator started in 2020 when our founder — frustrated with cluttered, slow, and inaccurate online calculators — decided to build something better. What began as a simple EMI calculator grew into a platform trusted by millions of Indians every month.

Today, our team of financial analysts, developers, and designers work together to ensure every tool is accurate, fast, and beautiful. We update our calculators with every RBI rate change, every budget announcement, and every tax reform.

2020
Founded & Launched EMI Calculator

Started with a single, clean EMI calculator. Reached 10,000 users in the first month.

2021
Expanded to 25 Finance Tools

Added SIP, FD, PPF calculators and reached 500,000 monthly users.

2022
Launched Health & Unit Converters

Expanded beyond finance. Crossed 1 million monthly users.

2023
Blog & Educational Content

Launched our financial literacy blog. 3 million monthly users.

2025
100+ Tools, 5M+ Users

India's most trusted free calculator platform. Growing every day.
